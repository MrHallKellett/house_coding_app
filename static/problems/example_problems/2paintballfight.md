Example Round 2 Problem C - Paintball Fight (**)

Henry Kellett is playing a paintball fight with the other admirals. They fight in a 1v1 format, where during every round as many pairs as possible play each other simultaneously, and the loser is eliminated. How many rounds will they need to play before they determine an overall winner?

Input Format

A single positive integer: the number of people Henry will play with.

Output Format

A single integer: the number of rounds they can play.

Example 1

Input:

10

Output:

4

Example 2

Input:

30

Output:

5

Note: When taking input, do not print any message. For example, input() or input(““) would be fine, but input(“Number: “) or input(“Something“) might fail the tests.
