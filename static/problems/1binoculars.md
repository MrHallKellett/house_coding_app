# Problem 31 - Binoculars (*)

<img src="/static/problems/1binoculars.png" width="300"/>

Henry is looking through his binoculars, which can always see exactly three characters at any one time.

What is in the middle of the sea?

## Input Format

A string, representing the contents of the sea. The sea is guaranteed to have an odd length, and be at least three characters long.

## Output Format

Three characters, representing the contents of the middle of the sea.

## Example 1

### Input:

    🌊🐳🐋🐬🦈🐙🦑

### Output:

    🐋🐬🦈

## Example 2

### Input:

    🐙🐙🐙🐙🦑🐙🦑🐙🦑🐙🐙🐙🐙

### Output:

    🐙🦑🐙

Note: When taking input, do not print any message. For example, `input()` or `input(““)` would be fine, but `input(“Number: “)` or `input(“Something“)` might fail the tests.
